Tacos al Pastor

A Quintessential Mexican Dish

Tacos al Pastor is a simple yet very flavorful Mexican street-food. It is a corn tortilla piled with marinated, charred, thin shavings of roasted pork. Along with the meat it is also topped with pico de gallo, pineapple, cilantro and lime juice. Although this might sound reminiscent of Lebanese shawarma, the meat for Tacos al Pastor is made out of boneless pork shoulder and not lamb.

This dish is very easy to make. It is recommended to serve it with warm corn tortillas. It can be made on the grill or in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 tomato
  • 3 dried guajillo chile peppers, seeded
  • 2 dried ancho chile peppers, seeded
  • 1 pineapple, sliced 3/4-inch thick
  • ½ cup orange juice
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • ¼ cup white vinegar
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 1 tablespoon salt, or to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 pounds boneless pork loin, thinly sliced
