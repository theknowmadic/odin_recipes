Roast Beef Dinner

Roast Beef: The Low and Slow Path to Delicious

Roast beef dinner truly is a timeless dish. It can be made with rump roast or sirloin roast. It is prepared by slow-roasting the meat. This process work wonders by turning an otherwise tougher cut of meat into a tender and juicy dinner the family will love.

It is not a complicated dish to make. With a good piece of meat and some simple herbs it becomes more tender and flavorful than anything you can buy at the store. Roast beef goes very well with gravy and mashed potatoes. The leftovers are great for sandwiches, too!

Ingredients

  • 3 to 3 1/2 pounds(1.3 to 1.6 kg) boneless rump roast (pick an end cut with a layer of fat, if you can)
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 8-10 slivers garlic (3 to 4 cloves, sliced in half or into thirds)
  • Salt and pepper
